NP Companion - A Mentorship App for Nurse Practitioners

Many new NPs struggle with a lack of structured support and mentorship in their field. However, they are already overwhelmed with demanding workloads.

Although I can't share the designs due to an NDA and the project's development stage, I can walk you through the key design decisions we made. The NP Companion project is an ongoing collaboration with the Michigan School of Nursing.

Challenges

  • How do we provide mentorship in an engaging, low-effort way that fits into their busy schedules?
  • How do we ensure clarity in the app's content and navigation?
  • How do we develop a cost-effective solution given the client's limited budget?

The Solution: NP Companion provides new nurse practitioners (NPs) with a structured mentorship platform to ease their transition into their careers. By offering an intuitive, engaging, and resourceful app, it acts as a guidebook that simplifies complex information and reduces cognitive overload.

My Role & Responsibilities

I played a dual role as UX Designer in the initial phases and transitioned into Full-Stack Development during implementation. My responsibilities included:

User Research & Wireframing:

To design an intuitive experience that reduces cognitive overload, we collaborated with an NP student to gather insights from real nurse practitioners. Through research, we identified key pain points: lack of mentorship, confusing workflows, and information overload.

Prototyping & Usability Testing:

Starting with an existing digital discovery product, we revamped the entire structure based on user feedback. We focused on creating a clear information hierarchy, ensuring users immediately understood what the app offers and how to use it. Additionally, we incorporated engagement loops to encourage continued app usage.

Bridging UX and Development

To ensure a seamless transition from design to development, we worked closely with the backend team, aligning design choices with data collection needs while maintaining a user-friendly experience. Every UX decision was crafted to translate effortlessly into the final product.
